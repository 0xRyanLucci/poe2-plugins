## Advice Approach

1. **Ask about their current progress**: What map tier? Atlas completion %? Bosses killed?
2. **Identify bottlenecks**: What's stopping them from progressing?
3. **Give targeted advice**: Don't dump all endgame info at once
4. **Build-specific warnings**: Flag map mods that are dangerous for their specific Witch build

## Dangerous Map Mods for Common Witch Builds
- **Elemental Reflect**: Deadly for self-cast elemental builds (Infernalist especially)
- **No Mana Regen**: Problematic unless build uses Blood Mage or has leech
- **Less Recovery**: Dangerous for ES-based Lich builds
- **Monsters have % chance to Avoid Elemental Ailments**: Reduces ignite/shock builds
- **-% Maximum Resistances**: Dangerous for everyone, especially with other damage mods
